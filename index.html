Fluid Space

Space is precious. The optimal use of space on the web involves taking care of content, design and legibility. By using the principles from Utopia, space is created and preserved without having to worry about breakpoints. Trusting the ebb and flow of the internet to find the best solutions.

Scaling fluidity

As a starting point for fluid space, Utopia has calculated different spacial values which scales within it's own size. These four boxes is sized large, x-large, xx-large, xxx-large. The gap between is set to medium.

Resize the browser window to observe the transition.

These sizes can be customized and used in any way needed to support the content, for example transition between xxx-large and small with minmax(). This gives flexibility and variation, and challenges designers to maximize the contents potential.
